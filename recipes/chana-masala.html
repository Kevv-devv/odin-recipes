Chana Masala

Description

This is a staple of Indian cuisine and one of my favorite recipes. It combines traditional Indian spices like turmeric, garam masala, ginger, garlic, coriander, and cumin seeds with delicious chickpeas and basmati rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans chickpeas, drained
  • 1 cup rice, rinsed
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • l tsp cumin seeds
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • dash of ginger
  • 1 tbsp coriander
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp Indian chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric

Steps

  1. Sauté onion, garlic, and cumin seeds in a little water or vegetable broth for 5 minutes using the sauté function of the instant pot.
  2. Add diced tomatoes, tomato paste, and spices; stir until thickened a little.
  3. Close lid and set instant pot to pressure cook for 20 minutes. Let pressure release naturally for 10 minutes.
  4. While waiting for pressure to release, cook rice using rice cooker instructions.
  5. Combine rice and chickpeas and serve!
