Black Bean Chili

Description

This is my black bean chili with sweet potatoes and corn. This is a very simple recipe that you can throw together in the instant pot in no time. The warm, sweet notes of the sweet potatoes are the perfect complement to the hearty beans and corn. Top it off with some delicious spices, and it's a bowl sent from heaven.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of black beans, drained
  • 1 bag of frozen corn
  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoons smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoons ground oregano
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 28 ounce can fire roasted tomatoes
  • 4 cups low sodium vegetable broth

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredient in the instant pot.
  2. Close lid and set instant pot to pressure cook for 20 minutes. Let pressure release naturally for 10 minutes.
  3. Enjoy a big, hearty bowl of delicious vegan chili!
